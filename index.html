ABOUT US

Welcome to the Margins Chess Club. Founded in 2024, our club is dedicated to fostering a vibrant chess community. We bring together players of all levels, from beginners to grandmasters, to share our passion for the game. Our mission is to promote strategic thinking and continuous improvement through regular meetings, tournaments and educational events.
